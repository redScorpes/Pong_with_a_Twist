## Pong with a Twist

This is my personal Twist on Pong with PowerUps, a Stamina-Bar and local multiplayer.

### Controls:

#### Player 1:
- movement: w and s
- sprint: space
#### Player 2:
- movement: arrow up and arrow down
- sprint: right shift
